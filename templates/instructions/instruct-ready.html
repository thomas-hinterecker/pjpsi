Instructions

You will assess 13 problems in total.

In each problem you first have to decide whether or not assertions follow of necessity from a premise, that is, given the truth of the premise must the assertion be true.

Secondly, you have to estimate probabilities of certain assertions.


Please read all of the assertions carefully! Take your time to think about your responses and do not merely guess.


As soon as you are ready to start the experiment, press the "Begin experiment" Button.
