Instructions (2/2)

You'll perform two different task in two seperate blocks. In each block you'll assess 13 problems. On each problem, you'll be presented with two assertions.

In one block your task will be to determine whether both assertions of a particular problem could be true at the same time. In the other block you'll be provided with a given probability for the first assertion of a problem and you'll have to estimate probabilities of the second assertion. Each problem will ask you to read the assertions before performing a task. Each block will repeat the necessary instructions.


Please read all of the sentences carefully! Take your time to think about your responses and do not merely guess.


As soon as you are ready to start the experiment, press the "Begin experiment" Button.
