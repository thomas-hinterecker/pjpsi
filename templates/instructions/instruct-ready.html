Instructions

You will assess 18 inferences in total. While doing so, you have to decide whether or not the conclusion follows of necessity from the premise, that is, given the truth of the premise must the conclusion be true.

After you have assessed an inference, you have to estimate probabilites of certain assertions related to the inference.

Please read all of the inferences and assertions carefully!


As soon as you are ready to start the experiment, press the "Begin experiment" Button.
